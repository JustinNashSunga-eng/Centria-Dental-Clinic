Services - Centria Dental Clinic

Oral Prophylaxis

Oral prophylaxis is a professional dental cleaning procedure designed to remove plaque, tartar, and surface stains from your teeth. This preventive treatment helps protect against cavities, gum disease, and bad breath, promoting long-term oral health. Regular oral prophylaxis appointments keep your smile clean, healthy, and confident.

Tooth Restoration

Tooth Extraction

Tooth extraction is a dental procedure that safely removes damaged or problematic teeth. This treatment helps relieve pain, prevent infection, and protect surrounding teeth, promoting long-term oral health and a comfortable, healthy smile.

Dentures

Dentures are custom-made removable teeth that replace missing or damaged teeth. This treatment restores chewing function, improves speech, and enhances the appearance of your smile, promoting long-term oral health and confidence.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is a dental treatment that corrects misaligned teeth and jaws using braces, aligners, or other appliances. This treatment improves bite function, enhances the appearance of your smile, and promotes long-term oral health and confidence.
